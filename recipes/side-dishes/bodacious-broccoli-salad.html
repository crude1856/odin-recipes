Bodacious broccoli salad recipe

return home

Description

This broccoli salad with bacon and cheese is requested at every family gathering. Let it be your next dish that they crave! I like this dish to be prepared at least two hours before serving. Be sure to have copies of the recipe on hand, as everyone will ask for it!

Ingredients:

  • 8 slices of bacon
  • 2 heads of fresh brocolli, chopped
  • 1 1/2 cups of sharp Cheddar cheese, shredded
  • 1/2 large red onion, chopped
  • 2/3 cup of mayonnaise
  • 1/4 cup of red wine vinegar
  • 1/8 cup of white sugar
  • 2 teaspoons of ground black pepper
  • 1 teaspoon of salt
  • 1 teaspoon of fresh lemon juice

Steps:

  1. Step 1:

    Place bacon in a large, deep skillet. Cook over medium-high heat until crisp and evenly browned, 8-10 minutes. Transfer to a paper towel-lined plate and crumble when cool enough to handle.

  2. Step 2:

    Combine bacon, broccoli, cheese, and onion in a large bowl.

  3. Step 3:

    Whisk mayonnaise, red wine vinegar, sugar, pepper, salt, and lemon juice together in a small bowl; pour over salad and toss to combine. Cover and refrigerate until ready to serve.
